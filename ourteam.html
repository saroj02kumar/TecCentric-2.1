Datin Mary Gomez

Legal Counsel

Mary has a wealth of experience in the preparation of documentation for financial institutions, developers, and individuals. Mary is also active in various organizations including the Sabah Family Planning Association, Sabah Nurses’ Association, and she is also the Chairperson for the Women and Children’s Rights subcommittee under the Sabah Law Academy.
